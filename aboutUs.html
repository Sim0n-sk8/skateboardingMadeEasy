More About Us

Hi my name is Simon im a skateboarder from Port Elizabeth

They idea behind Skateboarding made easy was to teach people in PE how to skateboard.

    What we strive for:

  • To grow the skateboarding community in PE
  • To teach the next generation how to skateboard
  • To help people learn how to ride a skateboard
  • To offer helpfull classes to the community

    • Our History:

    I started skateboarding in late 2018 after getting a longboard to skate along with my older brother.

    That longboard fortunately snapped in early 2019 and I got my first skateboard and was hooked on learning tricks.

    I quickly realized that doing this alone is really difficult as no amount of online videos can provide you with the help you can get in person.

    The same year I unfortunately broke my leg and realized that there is a gap in the community. We needed someone to help teach people how to skateboard safely.

    The need for a skateboarding coaching service was made very apparent.The community needed someone to teach how to skate safely and make it fun to learn.

    This is where the idea for Skateboarding made Easy came to life.

    Skateboarding made simple is there for those who need a little push (unirronically). For people who wants to start this amazing sport but are simply to scared to try

    Skateboarding made Easy was created to teach people of PE how skateboard safely.


    Vision:

    Our vision with Skateboarding made Easy™ is to grow the skateboarding community in PE

    We believe that there is a need for a skateboarding coach in the local area as most kids try and learn but get discouraged by the high skill ceiling of skateboarding.

    Here at Skateboarding made Easy™ we want to learn people the art of skateboarding and as such our vision is to educate, motivate and push the commuity while flooding in new skaters from all around PE


    Mission:

      Our mission is to:

  • Grow the community
  • Bring affordable skateboarding lessons
  • Educate new and old skaters on how to skateboard and teach them a big list of new tricks
  • Provide safety lessons to new skaters and skaters who are coming off of a injury
  • And most importantly keep on pushing!!

    • Goals:

    Our goals Skateboarding made Easy are to teach people how to skateboard and share the passion we have for it.

    We want to show the people of our lovely(and windy) city that skateboarding is a amazing sport

    Our goal is to teach people how to skate safely while having fun

    Our goal is to keep our class affordable so that as many people possible can learn without breaking the bank
