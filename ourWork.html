Our Work

Welcome to SkateboardingMadeEasy where we help you master the art of skateboarding, no matter your skill level

Here at Skateboarding made simple we offer skateboarding levels catered to any skill level.

We push to motive and progress any skater who signs up for our course.

With our range of classes we are sure that you will find exactly what you need.

We take extra caution to ensure your safety and that you wont learn any pesky bad habits along the line.

We believe that everyone can learn how to skateboard. Even if its the first time hearing about a skateboard we will teach you.

We strive to teach you all the tips and tricks to skateboarding.

Our courses

Beginner's

We take extra time and caution with beginner's as we understand it is a scary sport. We will go at your own pace and try to push you to try things outside of your comfort zone. At the end of the beginner course you should be well equipt to eithe move on to our intermediate course or to go try on new challenges on your own.

Intermediate

The intermediate course takes it a step up and our main goal is to teach you some basic tricks. We teach you some fundamental tricks and some just fun tricks. We teach you how to skate a bunch of diffrent obstacles and how to fall safely on all of them. After completing this course you should be comfortable on your board,understand how it functions and able to impress your friends with some nifty tricks.

Advanced

In this course we are fully aware that you are comfortable on your board so all we do is teach you so harder tricks. Everyone remembers their first kickflip...hours apon hours of trail and error. But in this course we try and teach you how to in the shortest time possible time We also teach you other advanced tricks and after completing this course you can go out into the streets and blow the socks of all your friends.

Custom

Our custom classes are catered towards what ever you want to learn. The only catch is that we must be able to do it. Try our coaches and see what tricks they can teach you.
